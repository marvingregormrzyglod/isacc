About ISACC

Seeing the Gospel Transform Society.

For over 40 years, The Institute for Studies in Asian Church and Culture (ISACC) has worked to see the good news of Christ take root in Asian cultures. We are a research and training organization dedicated to equipping leaders and empowering communities to build just and righteous societies.

Jesus is Lord of All Life

We work to advance God's rule in our national life, subjecting scholarship, media, and the arts to the scrutiny of the Word.

Witness Must Be Cultural

Following the incarnation, we seek to speak sensitively to a people’s ways of thinking and feeling to truly bring good news.

God is on the Side of the Poor

Conscious of God's focus on the poor, we work to empower the voiceless and address the injustices that create poverty.

Seeking Shalom for All

We are committed to the biblical vision of shalom—a world of peace and wholeness—working towards the restoration of all things in Christ.

Our History of Engagement

1978: Establishment

ISACC was established in response to the historical crisis of authoritarianism in the Philippines, beginning decades of advocacy and development work.

1986: People Power Revolution

Organized 'Konfes' (Konsensiya ng Febrero Siete), mobilizing a network of churches to serve as the center of evangelical presence at the EDSA barricades.

1990s-2000s: National Campaigns

Participated in major national campaigns, including the push for agrarian reform, the dismantling of US military bases, and the Jubilee 2000 debt cancellation movement.
