Building Leaders, Transforming Nations.

For over four decades, ISACC has provided foundational training to empower leaders who bring lasting change to their communities. Our courses are designed to integrate biblical truth with practical, real-world application, equipping the church to be a true agent of change.

GOSPEL, CULTURE AND THE FILIPINO CHURCH (GCFC)

For pastors and church workers. This seminar deepens cultural understanding for more effective witness and ministry within the Philippines.

TRANSFORMATIONAL DEVELOPMENT

For development managers and practitioners. A two-week intensive to apply Christian values in community development and poverty-reduction.

UNDERSTANDING INTEGRAL MISSION

For faith-based organizations. Learn the framework of Integral Mission and how to apply it practically in your development work.

CROSS-CULTURAL ORIENTATION (CCOS)

For expatriates and leaders new to the Philippines. Gain essential cultural intelligence to work effectively and sensitively in the Filipino context.

WRITING BIBLICALLY

For writers and media practitioners. A 3-day seminar to help you discern contemporary issues and write with a Christian mind.

WHOLENESS IN THE WORKPLACE

For churches and organizations. Discover the spiritual dimensions of ministry practice and bring restoration to your team and workplace.
