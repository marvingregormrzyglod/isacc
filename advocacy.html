A Prophetic Voice for Justice

ADVOCACY AND POPULAR EDUCATION FOR GOOD GOVERNANCE (PEGG)

ISACC's advocacy has been instrumental in mobilizing communities and shaping national conversations on justice and governance. From providing a center of evangelical presence during the 1986 People Power uprising to engaging in current issues, we are committed to being a voice for social responsibility.

Our work intentionally trains young people to become biblically literate and able to apply God’s Word to issues in the public square. A unique feature of ISACC’s educative and advocacy programs is the integration of Biblical perspectives into the analysis of complex issues.

Faith and Politics Initiative

A major initiative during the 2022 Presidential Election was a series of webinars on “Faith and Politics.” In partnership with Evangelical scholars, we aimed to help Christians become more socio-politically engaged in transforming our nation based on sound biblical-theological teachings. This collaboration with PCEC JPARCOM and Langham Publishing will result in a book designed to be a valuable contribution to the conversation on theology and politics in the Philippines and Asia.

Media Production for Social Change

Our multi-media shows serve as culture-friendly learning tools and artistic statements of our commitment to wholistic transformation. Among our productions are:

  • The Filipino’s Revolution: A 15-minute account of the February 1986 Revolution.
  • Statue Square: A 17-minute show on the situation of Filipina domestic helpers in Hongkong.
  • Onse de Mayo: A 15-minute documentary discussing personality-based vs. issue-based electoral systems.
