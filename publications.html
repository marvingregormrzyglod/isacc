Meet the Author

Dr. Melba Padilla Maggay

Dr. Maggay’s writings are essential for anyone interested in the intersection of faith, culture, and social transformation. As a social anthropologist with a Ph.D. in Philippine Studies, she brings a unique and deeply informed perspective to her work. Her experience as an activist, particularly her role in the 1986 People Power Revolution, gives her a credible voice on issues of justice, history, and national identity. Readers should be interested because she doesn't just offer academic theory; she provides wisdom forged from real-world engagement, making complex social issues accessible and relevant to people of faith.
